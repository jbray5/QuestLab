## Stripped 2026-07-15 (the over-specified first draft)

- **"Vow-Maker" + the 700-year backstory** for the being who merged the planes.
  Replaced by **the Weaver** (see the bible's DM-only canon): pseudonym used
  in-world; true name/nature unknown; merged the planes via pact; her essence
  *is* the weave; the shrines anchor it; she is fading. Everything else about
  her: uncommitted.
- **Hellbrim** — corrupted dryad, Halve's previous recruit gone wrong, wandering
  corruption-spreader who pre-seeded Wenneth's grove. CUT ENTIRELY. Canon now:
  **Wenneth turned because the severance unmade her.** The cipher hints only at
  an unnamed **"wrong hand"** — identity undecided (could become the second
  severancer, or something new).
- **Cael Amakiir as Halve's captive "asset,"** Cael-as-Halve-recruit-who-
  defected, the mentor-as-defector, and the **defectors' underground network**.
  CUT. Canon now: Cael is Fey, vanished when Thane was ~5, and is *somehow*
  connected to the severances — how is open.
- **Halve tipped Creed's investigation** of the corrupt cleric. CUT. Creed
  found it himself, until play says otherwise.
- **Halve routed Willa's compass to her / "she was walked here."** CUT. The
  black iron compass came off a ghost ship. That is all anyone knows.
- **The "halved soul" countdown** mechanic for Halve. CUT.
- **Willa as "the vessel" / Lay-of-Lanterns prophecy framing.** Softened to:
  she has an **unusual resonance with the light.** No chosen-one language.
- **Yelvyne's hidden journal + "three anchor-points failed this year."** CUT.
  She's a warm shrine steward, out of her depth on the cipher.
- **"Lantern Circle" as a player-facing order name.** Retired. Her order is
  unnamed for now: *"my sisters, scattered near the other lights."* One sister
  may get a placeholder name when actually met.
- **Four pre-named anchor regions** (Aldrum, Belling Stones, Pell's Hollow, …).
  CUT. Just "other lights, out there." Regions get named when visited.
