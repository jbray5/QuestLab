## ACT 1 — The Hearth · ~45 min

**Open:** Read silver-light arrival paragraph from runbook.

**Room setup:** Belva at bar (mead, both hands). Wenneth on a stool, leaf-ribbons in her hair, telling a joke. Lutenist in corner playing slightly wrong on purpose. Festival crowd. Shrine pulsing silver through the windows.

### NPC intros (~10 min)

- [ ] Belva pours, calls them "love"
- [ ] Wenneth waves with a wooden cup, doesn't fully look at any of them

### ★ HALVE ENTERS (~20 min) — five cuts, one per PC, **in this order**

> Halve walks in last. He doesn't sit. He moves between them like a chess piece picking its square. He speaks just loud enough that only the addressed PC hears him clearly.

- [ ] **Jenna (Warlock):** *"Your patron. She's been speaking to you since you were sixteen, hasn't she? Make a note of her voice tonight. You may want to remember it."*
  → seeds the silence she'll feel in Act 2

- [ ] **Parker (Rogue):** Halve says one Sylvan word — the name Parker's *father* called him. **Don't translate at the table.** Just say the word, let Parker react.
  → if Parker hasn't told you the word yet: improvise something soft (e.g. *"Aellim"*) and ask Parker afterward what it means to him

- [ ] **Chelsea (Nya):** *"Nya Ashwyn."* (beat) *"Forgive me — I should say it properly. Death's Shadow."*
  → if Chelsea picked one name only, use that one

- [ ] **Cory (Creed):** Halve names the corrupt high cleric Creed exposed. He says it with **respect**, like one notes the weather. *"[Cleric's name] still asks after you, by the way. He sends his regards. Such as they are."*
  → if Cory hasn't named the cleric yet: improvise (e.g. *"High Voice Vermenne"*) and ask after

- [ ] **Hayley (Willa):** Halve recites a single line of Lantern Circle liturgy — something Willa has read but shouldn't have access to as a non-member. He recites it like a benediction, not a quote.
  > *"And the bond that was kept in the dark was the bond that lit the world."*

### Halve exits

> *"There's something at the shrine that wants seeing. Walk over with the Priestess — I'll catch up. I do not like crowds at thresholds."*

- [ ] Yelvyne is already at the door. The PCs go to the shrine. **Halve goes upstairs to Belva's loft.** Note: Belva clocks where he goes.

## ACT 2 — The Severance · ~15 min

PCs arrive at the shrine. Yelvyne walks them through it — stone bowl, lantern-flame floating without fuel, the Chapel Oak (Wenneth's tree) visible across the clearing with yellowed leaves.

### ★ THE TRIGGER

> Mid-sentence. Mid-laugh. Mid-song.
> The silver light goes out. **Not dims. Not flickers. Goes out.**

- [ ] Belva's tankard hits the floor (heard across the green)
- [ ] Yelvyne's pendant cracks
- [ ] Wenneth screams — *not* a human sound

### ★ PER-PC FEELINGS — one per PC, while the world is still going dark

- [ ] **Jenna (Warlock):** her patron's voice goes silent. **Give her a private moment.** *"You have not been alone in your own head since you were sixteen. You are alone now."*

- [ ] **Parker:** the severance hits his body. Nausea, vertigo. Sound of something he doesn't quite remember closing. *Round 1 of combat: disadvantage on first attack/save.* Tell Parker out loud.

- [ ] **Chelsea (Nya):** she feels **nothing.** She notices that she's noticing. (No one else has had a normal moment in the last 30 seconds.)

- [ ] **Cory:** his Lay-on-Hands pool feels colder than usual. Bahamut is still there — but distant, as if Cory is hearing him through a wall.

- [ ] **Hayley (Willa):** the one Lantern Circle fact she remembers **clicks into place** as more than a fact. Ask her if she wants to say it aloud now or hold it.

## ACT 3 — Hold the Hearth · ~60 min

**Encounter:** "Hold the Hearth — Corrupted Fey Assault" (loaded from the HUD)
**XP:** 375 base · Moderate

**Roster:** Wenneth (Dryad statblock, corrupted) · Blossom-Seller (Bandit) · 3 Corrupted Pixies (Sprite)

### Round 1

- [ ] 2 Pixies through the broken tavern window
- [ ] 1 Pixie through the door
- [ ] **Parker: disadvantage on first roll** (from the severance pull)

### Round 2

- [ ] **Wenneth enters via Tree Stride out of a Market Green standing-stone** (a severed anchor-tree-turned-stone)
- [ ] Blossom-Seller walks through the tavern door with pruning shears
- [ ] Wenneth's Fey Charm: target the most-devout PC (Willa or Creed). **NOT to incapacitate — to plead.**

### ★ COMPASSION TRIGGER (any round)

> If any PC speaks **kindly** to Wenneth — *any* compassionate words, no check required — she **loses her action that round.** Her face contorts. She mouths "help me" before the corruption reasserts.

Tell players this is possible by having Yelvyne whisper it after Round 1.

### ★ WHEN THE FIRST PC DROPS

- [ ] Wenneth pulses sad-light
- [ ] All PCs hear this in their heads (Sylvan, but the meaning lands for all):
  > *"We are not the dark. We are what was left."*
- [ ] Wenneth keeps fighting after

### Round 3

- [ ] **Yelvyne arrives wounded.** She's been running from the shrine. She fights alongside if anyone is down.
- [ ] Dying shrine pushes one more line into PCs' heads (specifically aimed at Parker AND the Warlock — they understand it directly):
  > *"That is Hellbrim. We grew together for two centuries. Do not hate her for what is no longer her."*
  → This is **Wenneth's** voice mourning **the OTHER dryad** Hellbrim, who corrupted earlier. Use it to deepen the "she's a victim" reframe.

### Tactical reminders

- **Pixies** = harassment. Fly 30 ft, dart in, 1 dmg. **One hit kills them** — let martial PCs feel cool.
- **Blossom-Seller** = smart fighter. If PCs all go outside, she slips inside and threatens villagers to force a disengage.
- **Wenneth** = not tactical. In agony. Grapple on Thorn Lash hit. **Drawn to firelight** — does not chase fleeing PCs.

## AFTERMATH · ~45 min

- [ ] PCs go back to the tavern. **Master Halve is gone.** Loft empty. Cloak on the chair. Stack of old coin on Belva's bar.
- [ ] Belva: *"He paid in old coin. The kind that don't tarnish. Don't ask me again."*
- [ ] Hayley (if Willa knows it): the coin is from a dead kingdom from her reading

### ★ Yelvyne's offer

> *"I can reseal the shrine for one more night. The Lay-of-Lanterns. I need a witness. I need..."* (looks at Willa) *"... a vessel."*

- [ ] If **Hayley** wants Willa to be the vessel (Healing Hands): give her a CON save DC 12. Success: shrine relit through dawn. Failure: shrine flickers once, dies. **Either way, Wenneth wilts further.**

### ★ Wenneth's plea

> *"Don't. Let it go. The Feywild does not need a leash. Yelvyne loves it the way a child loves a sick dog — by refusing to see it suffer."*

### ★ THE CHOICE

- [ ] Yelvyne and Wenneth both look at the PCs.
- [ ] **Do not resolve.** End scene with: *"Sleep on it. Decide by dawn."*

## CLIFFHANGER · ~15 min

- [ ] Players go to their rooms. Belva offers blankets.
- [ ] **Parker** (alone in his room or hallway): something he keeps in his pocket — a memento — feels **warm.** It was cold every other night.
- [ ] **Jenna's Warlock** (in the dark): a single word she doesn't recognize, in the voice of her patron, faintly. Then silence again.
- [ ] Smash to black: dawn light hitting the shrine, which is dark.

## Things to remember

- **Halve's coin = dead-kingdom coin.** Hayley/Willa is the one who can recognize this.
- **The blossom-seller had a name in the village.** Belva will know it if asked. (Make one up: "That was Mira's daughter. Mira's daughter.")
- **Hellbrim** = a second dryad, dead. Wenneth's grief about Hellbrim is the moral pole's anchor.
- **Master Halve recognized something specific in each PC.** Suspicion: 100. Reveals: 0.

---

## If a PC dies for real

- 2024 RAW death saves: 3 successes stable, 3 failures dead, nat 1 = 2 fail, nat 20 = 1 hp back.
- Yelvyne can spend Lay-of-Lanterns on a downed PC for 1d6 HP back (DM call). Costs her ritual.
- If a PC actually dies and a player wants to roll a new one, **Belva has a son who was supposed to be a priest** — he can be drafted into the party next session.

## If you're running long

- Skip the lutenist bit. Cut Belva's "scarred ear" reference. Trim aftermath to 25 min.

## If you're running short

- Yelvyne offers a tour of the shrine BEFORE the severance: 10 extra min.
- Linger on Halve's exit — let players ask follow-up questions.
- Wenneth's healed-corruption moment after combat: stretch her dialog.
