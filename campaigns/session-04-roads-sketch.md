## The shape (from Arc 2 — "Other Lights", see the bible)

Whichever road they take, Session 4 is **a weird standalone destination**, and the
arc's fixed beats stay ahead of it: the cipher decode (midpoint reveal: TWO
severancers — the other one clumsy, leaving *"wounds that will not close"*), a
badly-severed town, a race to a live shrine, an arc-out letter from Halve.

Every road eventually reaches **a sister near another light** — she gets a
placeholder name only when the party actually meets her.

## 🛣 SOUTH — the Silverway

- **Committed:** Aldermarch (a day's ride), then the city. The rumor: **a hooded man
  paid passage south with untarnishing coin.** Thane's compass twitched this way.
- **To decide when picked:** what Aldermarch is like; whether the hooded-man thread
  is warm or cold when they arrive; where the sister is; the weird destination.
- **Feel:** the trade road — inns, carters, news, the first place the party hears
  what the world thinks happened in Hollowmere.

## 🌊 WEST — the coast

- **Committed:** Blackreef way; Willa's home waters; **the tide has been singing.**
  Willa's visions of the drowned city live out here somewhere, eventually.
- **To decide when picked:** the fishing village they reach first; what the singing
  actually is; where the sister is; how much drowned-city to show this early
  (answer: almost none — a note under the water, not a skyline).
- **Feel:** cliffs, salt, weather; the road where Willa is the local and everyone
  else is a landlubber.

## 🎪 THE WANDERING MARKET

- **Committed (from the bible's Arc 2 section):** a fey bazaar never in the same
  place twice; **the Peace** (no violence on market ground); **promises are
  currency**; a **scrivener** who reads old work for prices (e.g. the memory of the
  relighting night, or a favor owed) — a natural cipher-reader if they bring it
  here; a **bottled shrine-light** curio; **the road out is never the road you came
  in on.**
- **To decide when picked:** how they find it; what the Peace looks like when
  tested; what the scrivener charges *these* PCs; where the exit-road dumps them.
- **Feel:** curiosity bait; Nya's kind of wrong. Wonder first, wrongness second.
