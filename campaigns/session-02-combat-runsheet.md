## 🌳 WENNETH — Corrupted Dryad  *(the centerpiece)*
**AC 15 · HP 50 (dial) · Speed 30 · Init ~+2**

**Attacks:** Multiattack — **2× Rending Bark**, **+5 to hit, 1d8+3** slashing each.
*(Optional 1/fight: a grief-wail, 15 ft — Wis save DC 13 or frightened till end of next turn. Drop it if the fight's already hot.)*

**Behavior: NOT tactical.** She goes for the **warmest, loudest, most-alive** thing. No focus-fire, no smart positioning. Pure agony looking for the nearest life.

> **🔔 TRIGGER — hp_lte 25 (half) — fires ONCE:**
> The bark stills. The black drains from her eyes for one breath. She finds **Thane's** face. *"Ae'lim — you're... it's so loud—"* Then the corruption floods back and she's gone again. **(This is the whole emotional spine. Slow down. Let it land. Don't rush to the next turn.)**

> **🔔 TRIGGER — round_gte 5:** −2 HP at the start of her turn. The grove is dying with her.

**DEATH:** She lurches to the **dead tree by the graveyard** *(pick it now — circle it on the map)* and **roots into it.** Last words, quiet:
> *"The light was a cage. You don't know what you're protecting."*
**The tree stays. It's now a permanent prop on this map forever.** (If they ever learn to reverse a severance — it could bud.)

## 🌸 BLOSSOM-SELLER (Mira's daughter) — Corrupted Villager  *(the save-able one)*
**AC 12 · HP 24 · Speed 30**

**Attack:** **Pruning Shears**, **+4 to hit, 1d6+2** slashing.

**Round 1: she does NOT attack.** She walks in **calm**, drawn to the firelight like it's any other night. Confused, not hostile. **Give the table a beat to realize she's *wrong*, not *enemy*.** She only lashes out once someone moves on her / round 2+.

> **🔔 TRIGGER — hp_lte 5:** drops the shears, clutches her head — *"Please — it's so loud — make it stop."*

**✅ SHE LIVES IF** they bring her down **without killing her:**
- **Melee hit to 0 = knockout** (5.5e: attacker's choice). Cleanest path.
- **Net / grapple / restrain / sleep** also works.
She wakes tomorrow **remembering nothing** past the light dimming. Permanently changed — quiet, won't carry shears again. **Becomes the village's living testimony. REWARD keeping her alive.**

**If killed:** she's just dead. A festival vendor the party cut down. **Let it sit. Don't soften it.**

## 🧚 CORRUPTED PIXIES ×2  *(one-hit catharsis + your comedy valve)*
**AC 13 · HP 3 (one solid hit drops them — by design) · Fly 30 (hover)**

**Attack:** **Needle-Teeth**, **+4 to hit, 1d4+1** piercing.

**Behavior:** screaming little gremlins. Dive at the **most fey-resonant** PCs (Thane, Nya, Willa). **Swing on lantern strings, duck behind stalls and tables, screech overlapping.** *This is where the levity lives in a heavy fight* — make them ridiculous, then let a martial splatter one for a cheer.

**No trigger beats. They die fast and ugly.** After tonight, **no pixie is ever seen in Hollowmere again** — the villagers notice the silence within a week.

# ⚖️ DANGER DIAL (keep it scary, not a TPK)

- Wenneth hits hard (~15/round on one target). **A downed PC is drama, not failure.** Creed has Lay on Hands; death saves recover.
- **Built-in mercy:** because Wenneth is **non-tactical and drawn to the loud/warm/alive**, she naturally turns *away* from a downed (quiet, fading) PC toward the active ones. Being downed isn't a death spiral here — it's in-character AND merciful. Use it.
